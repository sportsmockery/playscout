## 1. System Reference

### Numbering
- **First digit = ball carrier.** `1` = QB · `2` = RB (primary) · `5`/`6` = jet/motion back.
- **Second digit = hole.** Odd = left, even = right:
  `1` A-left · `2` A-right · `3` B-left · `4` B-right · `5` C-left · `6` C-right · `7` edge-left · `8` edge-right.
- **Pass calls = [receiver #] + route.** Receivers `3 · 4 · 5 · 6` (`8` in Doce). Example: `6 Hitch 3 Arrow` = #6 runs hitch, #3 runs arrow.

### Cadence (trimmed 4 → 2 + change-up)
| Call | Snap on | Use |
|---|---|---|
| **On One** | "Bulldogs – Set – **GO**" | Default. |
| **On Set** | Snap on first "**Set**" | Tempo / no-huddle / draw them offside less. |
| **Freeze** (change-up) | "Bulldogs–Set–GO–GO–Set–GO" (go on 1 after 2nd Set) | Short yardage only, to draw the jump. |

> Dropped "On Two" — it duplicated On One and added false-start risk.

### Motion (unchanged — all 13U-appropriate)
- **Flip** — #2 relocates to mirror the formation out of the huddle.
- **Across** — named player motions to the mirror spot on "Set" (e.g. `3 Across`).
- **Jet** — named player motions in front of QB, crosses on the snap (e.g. `3 Jet`).

### Audibles
- **Red** = run check · **Purple** = pass check. Color tells the line which protection/scheme to reset to.

### Base blocking rules (the 3 taught schemes — keep)
- **ZONE:** step 45° playside, head across the first interior defender, work up on the 45° track. *Push the defender back.*
- **PASS (shield):** athletic stance, shoulder-to-shoulder, wait for the defender, **don't create a hole** up the middle.
- **TRAP:** the trapped man is left unblocked, then kicked out by a puller; everyone else down-blocks away.

## 2. The 15-Play Core

### RUN GAME (6)

**① 22 TRAP** — *base run, bell-cow*
- **RG:** down block — first man inside (B→A). **C:** block backside A gap (the puller's check man). **LG:** PULL right, trap the first defender to show past center (the playside DT, left unblocked). **RT:** down/inside, climb to playside LB. **LT:** hinge, cut off backside.
- **QB (1):** reverse pivot, hand to RB, carry out backside keeper fake.
- **RB (2):** aim right A gap, read the trap kickout, cut tight off the block.
- *Mirror:* **21 Trap** (LG↔RG roles flip).

**② 21 / READ** — *inside-zone read, the constraint engine*
- **OL:** inside zone left — playside reach, backside down & climb. **Leave the backside DE (right) unblocked.**
- **QB:** ride & read the unblocked DE. Crashes → **pull & keep**; sits/widens → **give**.
- **RB:** inside zone track, left A/B, one cut downhill.

**③ 22 CRUNCH** — *power complement off the trap look*
- **Playside RG/RT:** down block (create the wall). **Playside edge (wing/TE or C):** kick out the end man on the line. **LG:** PULL, lead through the 4-hole for the first LB. **C/LT:** backside cutoff.
- **RB:** downhill behind the puller, bang it off the double.
- *Add `76` tag* = protect backside (per v1.0 "23 Crunch 76" note).

**④ 26 TORNADO** — *explosive perimeter power (off jet influence)*
- Pin-and-pull to the right edge (hole 6). **Playside:** pin the first down lineman inside. **Backside G + edge:** pull and lead to the perimeter (first double the end briefly, then climb to pitch/LB — matches v1.0 note). **RB:** press C gap, bounce off the pin.
- **Simon (backside slot):** one-on-one with the ILB — seal.

**⑤ JET SWEEP** — *tempo / perimeter answer*
- **Motion man (5/6):** jet, take the handoff crossing the QB.
- **OL:** reach playside, backside cut off. **Playside WRs:** stalk/crack the force defender.
- **QB:** catch snap, mesh on jet, carry out backside boot fake.

**⑥ QB DRAW** — *change-up vs an aggressive pass rush*
- **OL:** show pass set (invite the rush up the field), then log/seal the ends. **C:** climb to the Mike.
- **QB:** sell the drop 1–2 hitches, then draw straight up the middle behind the climb.

### RPO / CONSTRAINTS (2 — cap 2 live reads per game plan)

**⑦ FAKE 21 18 BUBBLE** — *inside-zone RPO*
- **OL:** run block inside zone (stay ≤1 yd downfield — keep it legal).
- **QB:** ride the zone fake, read the overhang/flat defender. Sits inside → **throw the bubble**; widens with the bubble → **give the zone**.
- **Bubble slot (3/5):** bubble path, catch, get vertical now.

**⑧ FLIP 28 6 RPO** — *edge-run RPO*
- Run action to the edge (hole 8); **QB reads the flat/second-level defender** and throws the quick **#6** route if he triggers on the run.

### QUICK GAME (2)

**⑨ 34 ARROW HITCH** — *high-low the flat*
- **#3:** arrow to the flat (~5 yds, gaining width). **#4:** hitch (5–6 yds, sit). **#6:** vertical clear (hold the safety). **#5:** backside hitch/slant (built-in hot).
- **QB:** 3-step. Flat defender widens with arrow → throw **hitch**; sits → throw **arrow**.
- **OL:** shield pass pro (big-on-big, RB scans).

**⑩ TWINS CORNER HITCH** — *corner/hitch high-low*
- **Outside #4:** corner (break at 8–10). **Slot #3:** hitch at 5–6.
- **QB:** hitch vs off/soft coverage; corner vs press/squat. Ball out on rhythm.

### SHOTS (2)

**⑪ VERTICALS (4 verts)** — *MOF read*
- Outside on the numbers (go), slots on the seams (bend to the hash). **QB reads the middle safety:** 1-high → throw a **seam** in the void; 2-high → best **outside go**.
- **Protection:** 5-man slide, RB checks the backside edge.

**⑫ FLIP 5 FADE** — *one-shot isolation*
- Isolate **#5** on a fade/back-shoulder. Pre-snap leverage read: press → back-shoulder, off → nothing there, **check it down** to the RB. One read, then protect the ball.

### HEAVY (1)

**⑬ BEAST 26** — *short-yardage & goal line*
- Heavy set, extra hat playside. **Playside:** DOUBLE-team the 3-tech/DE at the point, drive. **Backside:** cut off. **RB:** downhill hole 6, one cut behind the double. **Freeze cadence** here to steal a yard.

### GADGET (2 — one call per game, max)

**⑭ DOCE CROSS RETURN PASS** — *double pass (trick)*
- QB throws a clear **backward** pass to **#8**, who resets and throws downfield to the crosser. **Coach the backward pass hard** — if it's forward and incomplete it's just an incompletion, but a fumble on a lateral is live. Once a game, in plus territory.

**⑮ DOCE JET WHEEL HITCH POST** — *situational shot from Doce*
- Jet motion holds the flat; **wheel** up the sideline behind it, **hitch** underneath, **post** as the alert vs single-high. QB works wheel→hitch, post only if the middle is wide open.

## 3. Special Teams (NEW — was missing entirely)

> ~⅓ of your snaps. Install 10 min every practice from Week 1.

**PUNT — Spread / Shield (safest for youth)**
- 3-man shield 6–7 yds in front of the punter; everyone else on the line **big-on-big, inside-out, protect the middle, release on kick**. Two gunners release immediately to cover.
- Rule: **no one leaves until the ball is kicked** except gunners. Snapper is never a blocker on the first level.

**PAT / FIELD GOAL — Tight protection**
- Linemen in tight splits, **inside foot back, hinge protection** (no one steps to open a gap). Wing/upback seals the edge. Hold–spot–kick timing repped daily.

**KICKOFF — Contain & lanes**
- 5 across each side, **stay in your lane**, L5/R5 are the contain/safety men — never let a return outside them. Squeeze the wall, one safety stays home.

**KICKOFF RETURN — Middle return / wall**
- Front line forms a wall, retreat and turn to wall off the coverage; return man reads the wall.
- ⚠️ **Wedge rules vary by league — confirm your league's blocking-wedge restriction (often ≤2 in the wedge) before installing. Never coach a full wedge if your league bans it.**

## 6. Safety Notes (13U tackle — enforce)

- **Tackling:** teach heads-up / shoulder-leverage tackling only. **No Oklahoma drill, no Bull-in-the-Ring, no board/collision drills** — banned.
- **Head contact:** any player showing head-contact symptoms follows concussion protocol immediately — out until cleared. Post it in the practice plan.
- **EAP:** review the Emergency Action Plan at the first practice of the season and keep it with the field kit.
- **Reps over scheme:** if a play can't get 8–10 good reps a week, it doesn't make the game plan. A smaller menu, repped clean, is the whole point of v2.0.
