System Reference

Numbering

First digit = ball carrier · second digit = hole.

Carrier: 1 = QB · 2 = RB · 5/6 = jet back

1 A-L2 A-R3 B-L4 B-R 5 C-L6 C-R7 edge-L8 edge-R

Pass calls: [receiver #] + route. Receivers 3·4·5·6 (8 in Doce). e.g. “6 Hitch 3 Arrow”.

Cadence (trimmed 4 → 2 + change-up)

CallSnap onUse
On One“Bulldogs–Set–GO”Default
On Setfirst “Set”Tempo / no-huddle
Freezego on 1 after 2nd “Set”Short yardage — draw the jump

Dropped “On Two” — duplicated On One, added false-start risk.

Motion & Audibles (unchanged)

  • Flip — #2 relocates to mirror the formation.
  • Across — named player motions to the mirror spot on “Set”.
  • Jet — named player motions in front of QB, crosses on the snap.
  • Red = run check · Purple = pass check (line resets protection to the color).

Base blocking schemes

  • Zone — step 45° playside, head across first interior defender, work up. Push him back.
  • Pass (shield) — athletic, shoulder-to-shoulder, wait for the defender, don’t create a hole.
  • Trap — trapped man is left unblocked, then kicked out by a puller; everyone else down-blocks away.

Special Teams

NEW — was missing · ~⅓ of your snaps

Punt — Spread / Shield

3-man shield 6–7 yds ahead of the punter. Line blocks big-on-big, inside-out, protect the middle, release on the kick. Two gunners release immediately.

Rule: no one leaves until the ball is kicked (except gunners). Snapper is never a first-level blocker.

PAT / Field Goal — Tight

Tight splits, inside foot back, hinge protection — nobody steps to open a gap. Wing/upback seals the edge. Rep hold–spot–kick timing daily.

Kickoff — Contain & Lanes

5 across each side, stay in your lane. L5/R5 are contain/safety — never let a return outside them. One safety stays home.

Kickoff Return — Wall

Front line retreats and turns to wall off coverage; returner reads the wall.

⚠ Confirm your league’s wedge rule (often ≤2) before installing. Never coach a banned wedge.
